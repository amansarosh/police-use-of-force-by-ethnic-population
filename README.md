# Police Use of Force and County Diversity in Washington State

## Project Overview

This project investigates the relationship between police use of force and demographic diversity across Washington State counties. By combining official police reporting data with U.S. Census demographic information, we examine whether patterns in law enforcement practices correlate with community racial composition and diversity levels.

### Research Question

**Do counties with different racial and ethnic compositions show different patterns in police use of force?**

## About and Why

The data is a comprehensive overview of Washington State's county-level demographics by race alongside police use-of-force incidents. Together, these datasets provide the necessary context to examine whether higher levels of police force are associated with specific demographic characteristics and whether racial composition within counties correlates with increased policing intensity. By combining demographic and policing data, we can identify potential patterns of systemic disparities or disproportionate enforcement across different communities.
